# Glossary

## Opportunity
A candidate AI use case, initiative, pilot, or workflow improvement that may deserve funding, fixing, deferment, or cancellation.

## Value lever
The way an opportunity creates value: revenue uplift, cost reduction, productivity, risk reduction, compliance improvement, customer experience, speed, quality, or resilience.

## KPI
A measurable indicator used to validate the value of an opportunity.

## Evidence
A source-backed fact from a document, interview, spreadsheet, system extract, or user input.

## Assumption
A required input that is not yet proven by evidence.

## Inference
A reasoned conclusion produced from evidence and assumptions.

## Confidence
The degree to which a value, recommendation, or score is supported by evidence.

## Readiness
The degree to which an opportunity is ready to proceed based on data, workflow, owner, technology, and controls.

## Fundability
A composite view of whether an opportunity deserves investment now.

## Decision state
Fund Now, Fix First, Defer, Stop, Explore Further, or Unreviewed.

## Deployment boundary
The environment in which client data, documents, embeddings, prompts, outputs, and logs are allowed to reside and be processed.

## RAG
Retrieval-augmented generation: using indexed source documents and structured data to ground AI outputs in evidence.
