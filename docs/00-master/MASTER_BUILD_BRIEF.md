# Master Build Brief

## Product name

AI Value Discovery Platform

## Company context

Mal7 is a senior-led AI and data consultancy for GCC enterprises. The product supports Mal7's consulting motion by turning AI demand into governed, financially defensible investment decisions. The platform should be usable internally by Mal7 consultants and externally by enterprise clients.

## One-line product definition

AI Value Discovery is an enterprise AI investment decision platform that turns scattered AI ideas, pilots, documents, workflows, and pain points into a ranked, governed, financially defensible AI opportunity portfolio.

## Product promise

Most enterprises do not need more AI ideas. They need a disciplined way to decide which AI opportunities deserve money, which need fixing first, which should be deferred, and which should be stopped.

## What the platform does

1. Captures AI opportunities from manual inputs, documents, workshops, transcripts, and existing backlogs.
2. Normalizes each opportunity into a consistent business case structure.
3. Maps opportunities to workflows, KPIs, value levers, data assets, systems, owners, and controls.
4. Scores opportunities across value, readiness, risk, feasibility, evidence, adoption, and strategic alignment.
5. Recommends Fund Now, Fix First, Defer, Stop, or Explore Further.
6. Generates executive packs, pilot charters, portfolio heatmaps, blocker registers, and roadmaps.
7. Preserves evidence and assumptions so recommendations are traceable.
8. Supports GCC enterprise data-residency and client-hosted deployment requirements.

## What the platform is not

- A generic AI strategy chatbot.
- A deck generator pretending to be a product.
- A “replace consultants” toy.
- A project management system.
- A vendor marketplace.
- A prompt library.
- A random document Q&A tool.
- A consumer SaaS app.

## Target clients

Banks, insurers, public sector entities, semi-government entities, energy companies, transport/infrastructure entities, telecoms, large family groups, and large enterprise groups in the GCC.

## Deployment principle

The product is browser-based, but deployment must be flexible: Mal7 Demo Cloud, Mal7 Managed Single Tenant, Client Cloud/VPC Deployment, and Restricted/Air-gapped Deployment.

## Pre-MVP principle

The first build should be a local internal demo that runs without paid services, while preserving production-shaped architecture.

## Non-negotiable product rules

1. No funding recommendation without KPI, owner, data path, and evidence/confidence treatment.
2. Every AI-generated output must show whether it is extracted evidence, inferred assumption, or generated recommendation.
3. Every score must be explainable.
4. Every decision must be auditable.
5. Human review is required before final decision-pack generation.
6. Tenant isolation is mandatory.
7. Data-residency posture must be visible in the product.
8. UI must be enterprise-grade, restrained, and serious.

## Ethical adoption loop

The product should become valuable because it becomes the system of record for AI demand, value hypotheses, funding readiness, control blockers, decision history, pilot roadmap, and portfolio status. Avoid dark patterns. Build trust, accountability, and decision memory.
