# Product Vision

AI Value Discovery becomes the enterprise system of record for AI investment decisions.

## Problem

Enterprises have too many disconnected AI ideas, weak KPI linkage, unclear data readiness, late control blockers, pilots that fail to scale, and board pressure without investment evidence.

## Wedge

The product does not sell “more AI.” It sells better AI funding decisions.

## Category

AI Investment Decision Platform.

## Long-term ambition

Discover opportunities, score/prioritize, build business cases, define pilot charters, track outcomes, compare expected vs realized value, govern the AI portfolio, and build anonymized sector intelligence over time.

## Product principles

Value before technology. Evidence before opinion. Control before scale. Portfolio before pilots. Human judgment over blind automation. Enterprise trust over viral gimmicks.
