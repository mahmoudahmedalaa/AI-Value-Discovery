# Product Requirements Document

## Summary

Browser-based enterprise platform that identifies, normalizes, scores, prioritizes, and packages AI opportunities into funding decisions and pilot charters.

## Goals

- Structured AI opportunity backlog.
- Manual and document-driven intake.
- KPI/value/data/control mapping.
- Methodology-driven scoring.
- Portfolio decision recommendations.
- Executive decision outputs.
- Data residency and client-hosted deployment posture.

## Non-goals for pre-MVP

Real LLM dependency, production SSO, paid cloud deployment, live enterprise integrations, multi-user collaboration, mobile app, full air-gapped deployment.

## Functional requirements

- Workspaces with sector, objectives, deployment/data policy.
- Opportunity CRUD.
- Document library and upload path.
- Mock/real AI extraction via provider gateway.
- Scoring engine and explanation.
- Portfolio cockpit.
- Decision-pack preview/export.
- Audit log.
- Security posture page.

## Non-functional requirements

Local demo reproducible, desktop-first responsive UI, tenant/workspace separation, structured logging, tests, no hidden external calls in demo mode.

## Success metrics

Internal demo can show an end-to-end story in under 10 minutes and looks credible to Mal7 partners. MVP can support a paid discovery sprint.
