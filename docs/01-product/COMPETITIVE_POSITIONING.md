# Competitive Positioning

## Positioning statement

AI Value Discovery is an enterprise AI investment decision platform, not a generic AI consultant chatbot.

## Alternatives

- Strategy consultants: credible but manual and not persistent.
- AI consultancies: technical but often weak on value/governance.
- Generic AI chat tools: flexible but unstructured and unaudited.
- Innovation platforms: good intake but not AI-specific.
- Vendor assessment tools: vendor-focused, not value-first.
- AI strategy chatbot products: fast but often too generic for regulated enterprises.

## Winning line

Most companies do not need more AI ideas. They need to decide which AI ideas deserve money.
