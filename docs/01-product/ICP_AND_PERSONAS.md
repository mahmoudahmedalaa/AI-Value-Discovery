# ICP and Personas

## Ideal customer profile

Large GCC enterprise with AI pressure, multiple ideas/pilots, regulated or semi-regulated data, and budget for discovery/pilot scoping/platform access.

## Primary sectors

Banking, insurance, public sector, semi-government, energy, telecom, transport/infrastructure, large family groups, healthcare, real estate.

## Personas

### CEO / ExCo sponsor
Needs portfolio-level confidence and clear funding decisions.

### Chief Strategy Officer
Needs structured opportunity backlog, prioritization, and executive narrative.

### CIO / CTO
Needs data/system dependencies and realistic implementation path.

### CDO / Chief AI Officer
Needs governed enterprise AI demand pipeline.

### CFO
Needs value ranges, assumptions, sensitivity, payback, and confidence.

### Risk / Compliance / Legal
Needs control mapping, data transfer risk, privacy, human-in-loop, and auditability.

### Business unit leader
Needs practical pilot charters tied to workflow pain.

### Mal7 consultant/admin
Needs structured methodology, AI-assisted extraction, and faster decision-pack generation.
