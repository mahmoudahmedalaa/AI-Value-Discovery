# Risk and Control Framework

## Categories

Privacy, cybersecurity, model risk, regulatory risk, operational risk, bias/fairness, human-in-the-loop, auditability, vendor risk, and data residency.

## Required controls

Human review, approvals, audit logging, data minimization, access control, output review, monitoring, escalation, retention/deletion, model/provider restrictions.

## High-risk examples

Automated credit decisions, customer eligibility, medical recommendations, security/defense workflows, adverse employment decisions, and customer-facing advice without review.
