# Opportunity Taxonomy

## Categories

Customer service, sales/RM, marketing, risk/compliance, fraud/AML, operations automation, knowledge management, software engineering, finance/tax, HR, procurement, data quality, claims, citizen services, field operations, predictive maintenance, legal/contracts, executive decision support, product/pricing, supply chain/logistics.

## Patterns

- Copilot: assists human workflow.
- Automation: completes task/sub-process.
- Decision intelligence: improves prioritization/recommendations.
- Knowledge retrieval: grounded Q&A/synthesis.
- Monitoring/detection: anomalies/risks/events.
- Generation: content/code/document generation.

## Anti-patterns

Generic enterprise chatbot, no KPI, no owner, vendor-led pilot with unclear value, high-risk automation without audit, copilot with no adoption path.
